  KATAKANA SCRIPT


Katakana (カタカナ) - Hard script, is one component of Japanese Writing System.

Katakana is used to transcript foreign-language words into Japanese, writing loans word, emphasize, and so on... Katakana is similar to Hiragana in some way. It will be easier for you to study this kana after "mastered" Hiragana.
